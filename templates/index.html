How does it work?

  1. Find the recipe you like.

Go on... Give it a go!

But why?

Anything posted to the internet is inherently ephemeral. You can bookmark something you like, but there's no guarantee it will be preserved.

Whether the recipe is deleted to make way for a new one, or the recipe is changed without a mention, it's frustrating to lose a recipe you love.

So that's why we made this website, so you never have to again.
